Timeline

Know what to do. Simply.

No distraction.

Timeline is simple, it just help you plan your week without distraction. There is no weird and complex options, just 3 things to fill and you're done. Timeline is just what you should expect from a todo app.

Available everywhere

Timeline is available on iOS, watchOS, macOS and on the web. Which mean that you can use Timeline on pretty much every single device you own.
